About Me

Electronics Engineer & Problem Solver

I am Madan kumar S, a final year Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Bangalore. My engineering journey has been driven by a deep fascination with how electronic systems work from transistor-level circuit behaviour to full-system embedded architectures.

My primary areas of expertise include VLSI design, where I have worked on digital circuit synthesis and optimisation, and embedded systems, where I have developed firmware for microcontroller-based solutions across IoT, robotics, and automation domains.

I believe in building things not just studying them. Each project is approached with professional rigour: from requirements analysis and schematic design, through to firmware implementation and testing. My goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical electronics and deployable real-world systems.

"Engineering is the closest thing to magic that exists in the world and I intend to spend my career proving that."
